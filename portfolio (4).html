Sample bug reports

A few examples of issues I found during testing — the kind that hurt player experience and retention if they slip through unnoticed.

FPS Gun Test

Player clips into the wall near the staircase

Walking into a specific corner at the base of the staircase traps the player inside the wall geometry with no way out — they have to die or rejoin. Kills you mid-match at the worst possible moment.

FPS Gun Test

Frame rate tanks badly when explosions stack up

Multiple explosives going off together causes heavy frame drops. Aiming becomes nearly impossible and movement stutters. In a shooter where every millisecond matters, this basically breaks the match for everyone nearby.

FPS Gun Test

Reload cancels itself before it's done

The reload animation sometimes just stops mid-way through with no input from the player — leaving the gun empty and unresponsive. Happens most often while moving, which is exactly when you need the reload to actually work.

Mini Cup 2 Soccer

Ball passes through player

Sometimes the ball goes straight through a player instead of colliding properly. It looks wrong instantly and kills any sense of physicality the game is built on — if the ball isn't reacting to your body, the whole mechanic breaks down.

Mini Cup 2 Soccer

Delay when kicking the ball

There can occasionally be a delay between pressing kick and the ball actually reacting. In a game this fast-paced, even a fraction of a second off makes it feel unresponsive — especially when you're trying to score or clear a ball under pressure.

Mini Cup 2 Soccer

FPS drops badly in crowded matches

When a full lobby is active and multiple players are going for the ball at the same time, the frame rate drops noticeably. Movement and ball control become harder to read, which is frustrating in a game where split-second reactions matter.
